### ðŸ”¹ Conversion Process

The **Hybrid OTEC System** integrates two complementary cycles:

- **Freshwater Loop (Open-Cycle Element):**  
  Warm seawater is pumped into a **Flash Evaporator** under vacuum, where part of it **boils instantly** to form **low-pressure steam**, leaving salts behind.  
  This steam is condensed by **cold deep seawater**, producing **pure freshwater**.

- **Electricity Loop (Closed-Cycle Element):**  
  The low-pressure steam also acts as a **heat source** for a secondary working fluid, typically **ammonia**, within a **Heat Exchanger (Evaporator)**.  
  The ammonia vapor expands through a **Turbineâ€“Generator**, generating **electric power**.  
  The vapor is then **condensed** by the cold deep seawater and **recirculated** by a pump, forming a continuous **Rankine cycle**.
